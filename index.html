Noughts & Crosses

The winner is

User story 1

As two players of noughts & crosses, we are able to place either a nought or cross on the 3x3 grid by clicking on the desired square

  1. Clicking a square will place either a nought or cross
  2. Item (nought or cross) placed will alternate each turn
  3. No more than nine turns can be played
  4. A square may only be played once
  5. Noughts should go first

User story 2

As two players of noughts & crosses, if I click reset, the board is cleared, such that the game can start again

  1. Winner message should be cleared
  2. Noughts should go first
  3. After reset games should continue to meet acceptance criteria of user story 1

User story 3

As two players of noughts & crosses, if either places three noughts or crosses in a line, they are declared the winner

  1. A winner can be declared if a line fills any row
  2. A winner can be declared if a line fills any column
  3. A winner can be declared if a line fills any diagonal
  4. If all squares are used and no lines are achieved, then nobody is declared the winner
  5. If a winner is declared, then no further nought or cross can be placed until the board is reset as per user story 2
